What is Pomodoro Technique?
The Pomodoro Technique is created is a more productive way to work and study. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro, from the Italian word for 'tomato'. -Wikipedia
Features
  • Responsive design that works with desktop and mobile
  • Audio notification at the end of a timer period
  • Customizable timer intervals to suit your preference
Why this website?
This is a customizable Pomodoro Timer that works both on mobile as well as desktop browsers. The aim of this web-app is to help you focus on any task you are working on, such as study, writing, or coding. This app is inspired by Pomodoro Technique which is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo.
